Wimpy

Wimpy is a popular fast casual restaurant chain, known for its value-for-money meals and family-friendly atmosphere. It's a favorite for many, offering a wide range of options including burgers, all-day breakfasts, and coffee, with a strong emphasis on enjoying every moment. In Namibia, Wimpy has been a family favorite since 1989, serving up delicious food and creating memorable family experiences.
