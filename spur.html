Spur

Spur Steak Ranches is a South African family restaurant chain known for its warm, relaxed environment, generous portions, and a variety of food options, including burgers, ribs, grills, salads, and desserts. They have a reputation for providing value-for-money meals and creating family-friendly memories, with many local and international locations.
