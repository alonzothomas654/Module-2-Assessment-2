Checkers

Checkers is a South African supermarket retailer owned by sister retailer and discount chain Shoprite. Founded in 1956 by Norman Hertz Herber, the chain operates across Southern Africa. In September 2024, Checkers had 597 stores across South Africa, Botswana, and Namibia.
