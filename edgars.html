Edgars

Edgars is a Johannesburg-based chain of department stores, primarily operating in Southern Africa, including South Africa, Namibia, and Zambia. It's known for offering a wide range of clothing, shoes, home goods, and beauty products, and has recently shifted its focus to mass-market fashion and beauty, according to Wikipedia.
