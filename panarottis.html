Panarotti's

Panarottis is an Italian restaurant in Africa. It is known for serving pizza and pasta and is also vegetarian-friendly. The restaurant offers a range of meals, including dinner, breakfast, lunch, and brunch. Reviews mention that Panarottis is a popular, family-friendly restaurant with a playful atmosphere, including a kid's playroom and jungle gym. They also offer take-out and delivery services within a 6km radius of the mall.
