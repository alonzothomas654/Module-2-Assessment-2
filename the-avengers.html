MOVIE SUMMARY:

In "The Avengers," Nick Fury of S.H.I.E.L.D. assembles a team of superheroes, including Iron Man, Captain America, Hulk, Thor, Black Widow, and Hawkeye, to stop Loki and his Chitauri army from invading Earth and enslaving humanity. Loki, Thor's adoptive brother, seeks to rule the world, leading to a major battle in New York City.
