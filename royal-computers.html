Royal Computers

Royal Computers Namibia is an IT products reselling company primarily serving the African market. They offer a range of products including computers, accessories, printers, networking equipment, and software. In addition to sales, they also provide technical support. Royal Computers aims to provide end users with competitive prices and high-quality products from leading vendors, with most products carrying at least a 12-month warranty.
