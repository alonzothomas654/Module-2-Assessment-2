Woermann Brock

Woermann Brock is a prominent Namibian retail company established in 1894, operating a network of supermarkets and retail outlets across the country. They are known for providing a wide range of groceries, fresh produce, and household goods, with a focus on affordability and quality. Woermann Brock also emphasizes community empowerment, job creation, and supporting local businesses and farmers.
